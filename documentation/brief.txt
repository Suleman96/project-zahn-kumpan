# 🦷 Zahn Kumpan — Trial Project Brief

---

> 👋 **Hey Muhammad!** Welcome to the team trial. This task gives you a real feel for the kind of work we do at Zahn Kumpan. Take your time, think it through, and show us how you approach problems — not just the final result.
> 

---

## 📌 Context

**Zahn Kumpan** is a digital agency focused exclusively on dentists in Germany. We are building a **web portal** for dental professionals where they can:

- Read editorial blog content (public, SEO-indexed)
- Access **premium content** like provider comparisons (members only, behind login)

Our current setup is WordPress, but for the portal we are open to any CMS or tech stack. SEO is a top priority — we need to rank well on Google for dental-industry search terms. We will create a subdomain just like [portal.zahn-kumpan.de](http://portal.zahn-kumpan.de) for that so you got a complete new space for everything.

---

## 🎯 Your Task

You will deliver **two things**:

1. A **functional front-end prototype in the design of zahn-kumpan.de** (HTML/CSS/JS)
2. A **short Technical Concept Document** (1–2 pages, PDF or Markdown)

---

## Part 1 — Functional Prototype

Build a static, browser-runnable prototype. No back-end required. Use whatever stack you prefer (vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, React, Next.js, etc.).

## Pages & Features to Build

**A) Public Landing Page**

- Clear headline and short description of what the portal offers
- At least 3 blog post teasers (title, excerpt, date, category)
- A visible CTA: *"Log in"* / *"Register for free"*
- Correct semantic HTML (`<main>`, `<article>`, `<header>`, `<nav>`, `<footer>`)
- JSON-LD Schema Markup for at least one blog post (`BlogPosting` type)

**B) Login Gate**

- A login form (email + password)
- No real backend needed — hardcoded demo credentials are fine (`demo@zahnkumpan.de` / `test1234`)
- After login: user can access the premium area
- Before login: premium content is **visible but locked** — use a blur + overlay + CTA pattern (no full hiding)

**C) Premium Comparison Feature** *(only accessible when logged in)*

- An interactive comparison table for at least **2 fictional dental providers** (1. booking appointment and 2. Billing centre for dentists)
- At least **2 filterable criteria** (e.g. price range, support quality, feature set)
- Clean, professional UI — this is a product dentists will pay to access

---

## Part 2 — Technical Concept Document

Write a short document (English, 1–2 pages) covering the following:

## 1. CMS Recommendation

Which CMS would you choose for this portal and why? Consider:

- SEO capabilities (sitemap, meta tags, structured data)
- Developer experience
- Cost at scale (e.g. 10,000+ articles)
- Headless vs. traditional

Options to evaluate (pick one and justify, or propose your own): *Sanity, Payload CMS, Directus, WordPress headless, or other*

## 2. Auth & Membership Strategy

How would you implement the login/member system in production?

- Which service or approach? (e.g. Supabase, NextAuth, Memberstack, custom JWT)
- How do you protect premium routes server-side? (Important: client-side-only gating is not enough)

## 3. SEO Checklist

List your **top 5 technical SEO priorities** for this kind of portal. Be specific — not generic advice.

## 4. LLM Integration Idea *(Bonus — optional)*

How could an LLM be meaningfully integrated into this portal? Think about real value for dentists, not just a chatbot. One concrete idea with a short explanation is enough.
